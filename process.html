Our Process

No Pressure, No Obligation, Just a Clear Path Forward

Here's exactly what happens from the moment you reach out to the day your books and website are running like clockwork.

1
Day 1 · 20-30 Minutes

Free Consultation

We hop on a call or meet in person to learn about your business: what you sell, how you currently track money, what your website (or lack of one) looks like, and where you feel the most pain right now.

2
Within 2-3 Business Days

Custom Plan & Quote

Based on the consultation, we put together a recommended mix of services (bookkeeping cleanup, ongoing bookkeeping, financial reporting, website design, or some combination) along with a straightforward, no-surprises quote.

3
Week 1-2

Onboarding & Setup

We gather access to your bank feeds and accounting software (or set them up if you don't have any), and kick off the website discovery process if that's part of your plan. You'll have a single point of contact the whole way through.

4
Ongoing

We Get to Work

Clean books, a sharp website, and ongoing support, handled. You'll get regular updates and plain-English reports so you're never left wondering what's going on with your numbers or your site.

5
Month-to-Month

You Grow

With a solid financial and digital foundation in place, you spend your time running and growing the business, not chasing receipts or wrestling with a website builder. No long-term contracts; you stay because it's working.
