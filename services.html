What We Do

Bookkeeping, Reporting & Web Presence, Done Right

Every engagement starts with a free consultation so we can recommend exactly the mix of services your business needs, nothing more, nothing less.

01

Bookkeeping Cleanup & Setup

If your books are behind, messy, or nonexistent, we start by getting you to a clean, accurate baseline, so every report and tax filing from here forward is built on solid ground.

  • Chart-of-accounts setup tailored to your industry
  • QuickBooks Online account setup and configuration
  • Historical transaction catch-up and categorization
  • Bank & credit card account reconciliation
  • Cleanup of duplicate, miscoded, or missing entries
  • Hand-off documentation so you always know where things stand
02

Ongoing Bookkeeping

Once your books are clean, we keep them that way. Daily and monthly bookkeeping means you always have an accurate, up-to-date picture of your business, without lifting a finger.

  • Weekly or monthly transaction categorization
  • Bank, credit card & loan reconciliations
  • Accounts payable & receivable tracking
  • Invoice creation and payment follow-up support
  • Sales tax tracking where applicable
  • Year-end books ready to hand straight to your CPA
03

Financial Analysis & Reporting

Clean books are only useful if you can read them. We translate your numbers into reports and conversations that actually help you make decisions.

  • Monthly profit & loss and balance sheet reports
  • Cash-flow snapshots and forecasting
  • Expense trend analysis to spot where money is leaking
  • Margin & pricing review by product or service line
  • Budget-vs-actual tracking
  • Plain-English summary calls, no accounting jargon
04

Website Design

Your books keep the business healthy on the inside; your website is what customers see first. We build fast, professional sites designed to turn visitors into customers.

  • Custom, mobile-responsive design, not a generic template
  • Clear calls-to-action built around how customers actually book or buy
  • Local SEO setup so you show up in Baltimore & Pikesville search results
  • Contact forms, booking links, and click-to-call for mobile visitors
  • Fast load times and basic on-page SEO out of the box
  • Ongoing edits and support after launch, you're never on your own
